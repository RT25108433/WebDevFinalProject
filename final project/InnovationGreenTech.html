Green Tech

Green Technology and SDG 9 – None of the laid out goals of SDG 9, in Industry or Infrastructure, would be possible without advancement and innovations into greener, cleaner technologies. In fact, it is fair to say that some areas of SDG 9 entirely hinge on the incorporation of new technologies to replace older, less efficient and more environmentally damaging practices of years past. This link is laid out directly in one of the goals themselves, which highlights enhancing scientific research and upgrading the technological capabilities of all industrial and infrastructure related sectors, especially in developing countries.

Plans for Improving Green Technology – SDG 9 has a variety of initiatives in place for fostering innovation in the realm of Green Technology. These initiatives include encouraging countries to invest in innovation hubs, Research & Development Institutions and partnerships to support up and coming startups working in the Green Tech industry. To achieve this, as well as offering broader incentives, it encourages governments to implement policies such as tax incentives for green tech implementation and development, emissions regulations and enforcing sustainability standards.
